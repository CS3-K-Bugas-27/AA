MERCURY

Closest planet to the sun

  • Mercury has virtually no atmosphere, which means it experiences extreme
    temperature variations between its scorching hot days and frigid nights
  • Mercury has the shortest year of all the planets in our solar system, with
    an orbit of just 88 Earth days
  • Mercury is one of the two planets in our solar system with no natural moons
    or satellites
  • The planet's largest impact crater, the Caloris Basin, is so vast that it could fit
    Texas within its boundaries
  • Due to its thin atmosphere, Mercury experiences one of the most significant
    temperature extremes in the solar system, with daytime temperatures reaching
    up to 800 degrees Fahrenheit (430 degrees Celsius) and nighttime temperatures
    dropping to around -290 degrees Fahrenheit (-180 degrees Celsius)
