VENUS

hottest planet!

  • Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system, with surface
    temperatures that can reach up to 900 degrees Fahrenheit
    (475 degrees Celsius) due to its thick atmosphere that traps heat
  • Venus is home to numerous volcanoes, some of which are among
    the largest in the solar system. Maat Mons, for instance, is one of
    the most massive volcanoes on Venus.
  • Venus is often referred to as Earth's "sister planet" due to its
    similar size and location in the solar system. However, it is extremely different in terms of
    its surface conditions.
  • It takes about 243 Earth days to complete one rotation.
